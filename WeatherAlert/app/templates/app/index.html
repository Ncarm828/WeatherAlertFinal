WeatherAlert

Welcome to WeatherAlert! A free weather app that sends you morning texts with the weather

Getting started

First you'll need to create an account. Click Log in/Register -> then click Register. Once you fill out all the nessary information, you will then be prompted to enter a code. this code will send to you through a text. NOTE: All texts are sent through Twiliow. You must accept these terms in order to create an account

Account

Once your account has been verifed, you can click weather to see the current weather of the loction that you have entered within your profile. If for any reason you need to change the loction or time you recieve your good morning text, you can update this within the profile tab.

Last thoughts

For any reason you do not wish to contiune using this app, you can delete your account. All you need to do is click Profile -> Delete Account
